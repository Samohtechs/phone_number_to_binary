"Converting a phone number to it's binary is useful in scenarios where you have to place a phone number in form of binary instead of actual phone number. Example, when you intend to hack a WhatsApp group and become Admin you have to take admin's phone number and convert it to binary."

NB: Convertion is done per single digit, hence entering example, 25 will be seen as a 2,5 (which will output 00100101) and not a 25 as a whole (expecting to be 11111).
